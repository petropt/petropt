# Enterprise Support

petropt is MIT-licensed open-source software. Use it freely in commercial work,
consulting, research, and AI applications. No copyleft restrictions, no support
contract required.

That said — textbook correlations are the starting line, not the finish line.
If your team is solving real production problems and the open-source library
ends where your work begins, **Groundwork Analytics** is the team behind
petropt and offers paid services to fill the gap.

## What Groundwork Analytics offers

- **Custom correlations and proprietary models** — beyond textbook formulas:
  Bayesian decline-curve analysis, multi-well anomaly detection, fluid
  recommendation from compositional databases, completion optimization.
- **Field-specific calibration** — turn the generic `petropt.correlations`
  into a tuned model for your basin / formation / fluid system using your
  historical data.
- **Petrophysical and production data pipelines** — automated LAS QC,
  multi-well log analysis, real-time production surveillance.
- **Training and onboarding** — get your engineering team productive with
  petropt + Python in days, not months.
- **Reservoir-engineering consulting** — decline forecasts, type curves,
  matbal, RTA, EUR / 3P reserves work for review or independent execution.
- **Custom open-source contributions** — sponsor a feature in petropt that
  benefits your team and the wider community.
